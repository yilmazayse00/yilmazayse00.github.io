The Adana kebab is a kind of kebab made in Adana, with the name of "armor", made with a knife similar to the one on the shore. The most distinctive feature distinguishing Adana kebab from other kebabs is meat. Definitely not done with veal. It is made with only lamb, lamb tail, flaked pepper and salt. It is served with tomato and pepper, served with seasonal salad, mint parsley-ornamental pepper-lemon plate, onion salad and mash. Turnip is consumed with water, ayran or raki.
